# class-project-aeromatch
AeroMatch is an interactive web app that helps aviation enthusiasts and students discover their ideal aircraft, flight path, or aviation career match through a fun, personality-based quiz. From future pilots to aerospace engineers, users answer a series of questions and instantly see their personalized results.

# how-to-take-the-quiz
You will answer 10 aviation-themed personality questions.
Choose the option that best matches YOU.
The progress bar shows how far along you are.
Your final career match appears when you finish.
Matches are between Airline Commercial Pilot, Private Pilot or Aircraft Engineer.
